Last week I posted an introductory post on SignalR. The focuse was getting started with it. I used the concept of Hubs from SignalR. Hubs get you started very fast but also have a downside - the messages are encapsulated in an envelope, like:
[js]
{"hub":"chatHub","method":"Message","args":["my message"],"state":{},"id":1}
[/js]
